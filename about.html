About This Project

Kashmir Voices is a platform dedicated to sharing essays, stories, and creative content by Kashmiris worldwide. We celebrate the rich culture, history, and lived experiences of the Kashmiri people through authentic voices.

As a new platform, we are iterating quite quickly, and would love to hear from you regarding how this platform might best be used. Current thoughts include:

  • a platform to share funny anecdotes about Kashmiri idioms/aphorisms. E.g. in britain they say "oh he wants his bread buttered on both sides", in hindi they might say "दोनों हाथ लड्डू होना" or "[oh he wants] a laddoo in both hands". Might be fun / cute stories that are subtly telling regarding culture: in britain, they butter their bread, in india, they palm their laddoos
  • a series of rants written by KPs who often feel tokenized/disenfranchised, constantly misquoted, with this being a publically facing place to get perspectives directly from the source
  • your idea here

Whether you are a reader or a contributor, we welcome you to explore and share the diverse narratives that make Kashmir vibrant. And if you have ideas on what we are / what we should be, we would love to hear from you! Just reach out to arjun :)
